Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The process of creating and implementing new ideas, methods, or technologies to solve problems, improve efficiency, or enhance experiences.

Sustainability

The practice of meeting present needs without compromising future generations, focusing on environmental, social, and economic balance.

Infrastructure

The fundamental systems and structures, such as transportation, utilities, and technology, that support the functioning and development of a society or organization.

Empowerment

Providing individuals or communities with the tools, confidence, and opportunities needed to take control of their lives and make informed decisions.
